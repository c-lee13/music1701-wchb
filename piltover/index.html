What Could Have Been

Piltover

City of Progress

 

Gleaming with innovation, wealth, and technological marvels, Piltover -- a city in the vast League of Legends universe -- stands as a monument to scientific achievement.

But not all that glitters is gold. 

Piltover's social structure hinges on capitalism. Those born into privilege, wealth, and influence dominate every sphere, while the rest are left to navigate a system rigged against them.

Governed by a council composed of elite families, many of whom have held power for generations, Piltover is rampant with corruption. Consequently, new technological innovations -- more often than not -- serve not the public good, but the continue reign and profit of these elite. 

Moreover, Piltover's success breeds an air of superiority, inclining it to exploit its impoverished sister city Zaun. Despite relying on Zaun for cheap labor and resource extraction, Piltover continues to deny it political representation and basic infrastructure (e.g. proper sanitization, education, and medical care). In doing so, Piltover sustains its prosperity at the expense of Zaun, abusing it as a necessary but disposable pawn for its own advancement.

Decades of classist prejudice against Zaun have led Piltover’s citizens to become either willfully ignorant of its suffering or actively complicit in it -- their disdain for Zaunites shaped by the comfort and entitlement of their privilege.

Piltover’s gleaming façade conceals a rot of apathy, injustice, and classist delusion -- corruption that, ironically, feeds the very violence and immorality it claims to stand above.
