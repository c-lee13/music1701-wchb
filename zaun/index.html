What Could Have Been

Zaun

The Undercity

 

Zaun, the Undercity in Arcane, is a brutal world where survival of the fittest isn’t just a phrase—it’s a way of life. Beneath Piltover’s gleaming towers, Zaun is steeped in physical and social toxicity: even the air is poisonous, a constant reminder of the harsh living conditions and the resilience required just to survive. In this unforgiving environment, those who remain silent are trampled. To rise in the ranks, you must be loud, relentless, and willing to fight for your place.

Led historically by Vander and Silco—the brawn and the brains of the Undercity—Zaun’s leadership once balanced between moderate peacekeeping and ruthless ambition. Vander, a father figure to many, believed in protecting Zaun through restraint and diplomacy. In contrast, Silco advocated for radical independence, even if it meant bloodshed. Their split reflected two opposing visions: one of survival through stability, and another of liberation through upheaval. Silco’s use of Shimmer and violent tactics (as depicted in the “gassing the Undercity” video) exemplifies the extremes the oppressed will endure to be heard.

Zaun’s uprising—sparked by years of discrimination, exploitation, and broken promises—was brutally suppressed by Piltover, reinforcing the systemic power imbalance. This reflects what political theorist Kublawi describes as “dynasties and dystopias”: entrenched elites (like Piltover’s council) hoard power across generations, while marginalized groups must resort to desperate measures for recognition (Kublawi 2022). Zaun is thus not only a city of hardship, but also one of revolutionary spirit—raw, dangerous, and aching for change.
